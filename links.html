  • 3Blue1Brown - Essential for building deep, visual intuition in complex math topics.

  • Mathologer - Fun, accessible, and rigorous explorations of advanced and recreational math.

  • Numberphile - Features leading mathematicians explaining fascinating concepts about numbers.

  • Stand-up Maths - Matt Parker connects mathematics to real-world objects and comedy.

  • Veritasium - A top science channel that often covers profound mathematical ideas with incredible clarity.

  • Tibees - Solves challenging physics and math problems from top university exams like Cambridge and MIT.

    • Channels with Full University-Level Courses

  • MIT OpenCourseWare (Gilbert Strang's Linear Algebra) - A legendary and complete university course.

  • NPTEL - India's official source for courses from IITs and IISc on a vast range of subjects.

  • The Math Sorcerer - Focuses on self-study, with countless videos on specific topics and math book reviews.

  • Professor Leonard - Famous for his full-length, easy-to-follow lectures covering Calculus I, II, III, and more.

    • Channels for Problem Solving & Competitive Exams

  • MindYourDecisions - Presh Talwalkar solves popular math riddles and problems often seen in interviews.

  • blackpenredpen - High-energy videos focused on creatively solving integrals and other calculus problems.

  • Michael Penn - Excellent resource for higher-level math, including Olympiad problems, abstract algebra, and number theory.

  • Flammable Maths - Solves interesting and difficult math problems in a very clear, step-by-step manner.
